SEO Discussions

Micro/Macro Conversion Goals

Bounce Rate

Bounce rate tell us directly the degree of a user might be interested in my website based on my home page. For my website, the goal is to have a bounce rate that is less than 48% for now, since it is a brand new website. I would not guarantee that everyone would like my website from their first sight. It is also resonable to have a lower bounce rate than Lebronjames.com because my website has more than one informational page filled with contents, while lebronjames.com has very limited number of pages.

Data collected from https://www.similarweb.com/website/lebronjames.com/#traffic

Why is this important?
Whether or not the goal has been reached or not could imply if I am proven good enough or not in designing my home page. Is it designed well enough or informational enough to make the users stay for other inner pages?

Number of visits

Number of visits could tell us directly the number of users might be interested in me. However it is only meaningful if we compare the number of visists between each month.
For my website, the starting goal is at least 100 users for the first month and we could go from there. The next month, there would be fewer visists because most of the people has already visted in the first month, but people might revisit my website in the third month.
I am mimicking the pattern of Lebronjames.com since his website is also about him as a person and a professional athlete.

Data collected from https://www.similarweb.com/website/lebronjames.com/#traffic

Why is this important?
Whether or not the goal has been reached or not could imply if the marketing or advertisation is good enough generally. But for me, it could imply whether my website is good enough so users might share my website with others.

Average Visit Duration

By average visit duration, I want to measure how long user spent on my website. For my website, time spent on the home page could be ignored because the home page does not have so much meaningful information for my portfolio, rather it summarizes and introduces basic information.
My prediction is to have an average visit duration of about 40 seconds. It is slightly longer than lebronjames.com becauase I have more pages that contain more contents that might require some time for reading.

Data collected from https://www.similarweb.com/website/lebronjames.com/#traffic

Why is this important?
Whether or not the goal has been reached or not could imply if I am proven good enough or not in my professional field. Or is the website good enough to attract and keep people interested.

Segmentations

Geographic Location

Geographic location tells us where the users are distributed.
Based on the data from lebronjames.com, users are mostly from the US since it is the most popular country of the sport basketball which is what lebronjames.com is about. Lebronjames.com, therefore, posts information about business and sports based on the information in the US and for the US citizens as a priority. For my website, I would priortirize my users in Canada, since I live in Canada. For example, I will do so by stating my cooperation would priortirize companies in my local, while online coorperation would be considered when there is no prior local business or project requests.

Data collected from https://www.similarweb.com/website/lebronjames.com/#traffic
Why is this important?
This is important for me because I would want to do business or accept project request in the local as my priority. Therefore, it is important for me to post information or accept customers based on their geographic location.

User Interests

User interests could tell us what the visitors on the site are interested in, therefore website desingers could make adjustments based on such inforomation.
Based on lebronjames.com, we could tell users' interests mostly basketball and support foundations and donations. On the other side, my website focused more on website designing and myself, therefore, users' interests are predicted to be 'programming and developing software'. While other interests are not as priotirized.

Data collected from https://www.similarweb.com/website/lebronjames.com/#traffic
Why is this important?
This is important because of my main focus of the website being web designing and me as a professional. This cold be pre-set since the above reasons. Therefore, users with such interests would more likely to come to my website.

Source Types

Source types could tell us where the users might come from to a website.
Based on the data for lebronjames.com, their users mostly come from the search engine. But I predicted my users to come mostly from direct because not many people would know my website at this beginning phase, they would rather know it because of me sharing directly or indirectly.

Data collected from https://www.similarweb.com/website/lebronjames.com/#traffic
Why is this important?
This is important because a website would analyze whether the source of where users come to visit the website is good enough, or it requires more ways to share the websites, ultamately, making the website more popular.

Keyword and SEO Plan

The keywords: website designer, website design, UX Design, Junlong Ruan, David Ruan, Richmond Hill, Canada

I use keywords about website designers because I want people to know my website is about my projects about website design and UX design. I assume people most likely to search website design when they want to learn about it or find relevant designers.

I include my name as a few of the keywords because the website is about myself. This would be the most direct way to introduce myself to the public and let people know that I am a website and UX designer.

Moreover, I include locations because I want people to get the search results of my website when they search up website designer in my local area.
